How We Work

From vision to handover — and beyond.

A seamless four-phase journey that brings your project to life with full transparency at every step.

01

Discover & Brief

We listen deeply — to your vision, your constraints, your lifestyle — and translate them into a clear project brief.

02

Build & Execute

Manage & Protect

MPMS takes over post-handover — maintaining, managing, and maximising the value of your investment long-term.

Absolutely. Each VARAM company is a standalone specialist. You are free to engage just the architect, just the builder, or just property management. Working with all three together simply gives you a more integrated, seamless experience.
NRI clients are a significant part of our practice. MPMS provides regular photo and video updates, monthly financial statements, and a dedicated point of contact available across time zones. You always know exactly what is happening with your property.
We work across residential (independent homes, villas, apartments), commercial (offices, retail, mixed-use), hospitality, and renovation projects. Our portfolio spans compact urban plots to large ECR developments.
Shri Associates uses detailed milestone-based schedules and transparent cost tracking. Clients receive regular progress reports, and any variation to scope or cost is discussed and approved before work proceeds — no hidden surprises.
