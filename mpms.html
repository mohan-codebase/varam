What We Do

Property management for owners who cannot always be there.

A property left unattended deteriorates quietly and quickly. Hairline cracks become structural problems. Plumbing leaks become damp. Vacant properties become targets. By the time an absentee owner discovers an issue, it has typically become expensive to fix.

MPMS exists to prevent that. We provide regular physical inspections, clear photographic condition reports, prompt coordination of repairs, and a single, reliable point of contact who knows your property as well as you do — and takes the same care with it.

We work with individual homeowners, NRI families managing inherited properties, investors with multiple units, and landlords who simply want someone dependable on the ground.

MPMS is particularly valuable for:

  • NRI families with inherited or investment property in India
  • Property investors managing multiple units across the city
  • Owners who have relocated and cannot visit regularly
  • Landlords whose tenants have vacated and the property is unoccupied
  • Owners managing post-construction defect periods
  • Anyone who wants structured, documented accountability for their property

What We Provide

Property management services.

A structured programme of physical visits, condition monitoring, and coordinated upkeep — delivered with clear documentation at every stage.

Property Inspection

Regular physical inspections by our team — checking structural condition, security, cleanliness, and general upkeep. Frequency tailored to your property type and occupancy status.

Condition Assessment Reports

Detailed written reports with photographs documenting property condition after every inspection — giving you a clear, timestamped record you can act on from anywhere in the world.

Maintenance Supervision

We identify maintenance needs early, source reliable contractors, supervise the work on your behalf, and confirm quality before closing out any job — so you are never managing tradespeople remotely.

Coordination of Repairs & Upkeep

From a leaking pipe to a full repaint — MPMS coordinates all works, manages approvals from owners, obtains quotes, and supervises completion. All expenditure is documented and approved before commitment.

Periodic Property Monitoring

For unoccupied properties — scheduled visits to check that the property is secure, utilities are functioning, and no encroachment, damage, or squatting has occurred.

Owner Communication & Reporting

Monthly summary reports, urgent issue notifications, and on-call support for questions — keeping you fully informed without requiring you to chase for updates.

The Process

How we manage your property.

A straightforward four-step process — from your first enquiry to ongoing peace of mind.

01

Initial Assessment

We visit your property, document its current condition thoroughly, and agree on a management plan that fits your requirements and budget — including inspection frequency and reporting format.

02

Management Agreement

A clear written agreement setting out our responsibilities, your authorisations, reporting schedules, and the maintenance approval process — so there are no ambiguities.

03

Active Management

Regular inspections, condition reports, proactive identification of issues, and coordination of approved maintenance works — all managed by a named contact who knows your property.

04

Regular Reporting

Monthly summary reports delivered to your email — photographs, condition updates, works completed, upcoming maintenance, and a clear financial summary of any expenditure.
