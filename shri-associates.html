Execution Philosophy

We build the way the drawings intend — not the way that is convenient.

Shri Associates was built on a frustration that most owners share: contractors who submit impressive bids, then cut corners when no one is watching. We run our sites differently. Every foreman on a Shri Associates project knows that the architect's intent is non-negotiable — because we are part of the same family.

When Varam Architects designs a building, Shri Associates builds it. That means the person holding the trowel has spoken with the person who drew the line. Problems that would take weeks to resolve in a typical contractual chain are solved in hours.

For projects designed by external architects, we bring the same discipline: precision to specification, active communication with the design team, and zero tolerance for unexplained deviations.

What We Build

Construction services.

From ground-breaking to final coat — across new builds, interiors, and renovation.

Building Construction

New residential, commercial, and institutional construction — from foundation to structural completion. We handle civil, masonry, RCC, and finishing works as a single integrated scope.

Interior Execution

Precision interior works including false ceilings, flooring, wall finishes, modular joinery, and custom millwork — executed to the design drawings with attention to joint, tolerance, and finish.

Renovation & Repair Works

Structural repair, waterproofing, partial demolition and rebuild, and selective renovation of existing buildings — including heritage structures requiring careful intervention.

Quantity Surveying & BOQ Preparation

Detailed bill of quantities, cost estimation, and tender documentation — providing clients with transparent, itemised cost visibility before a single brick is laid.

Project Management

For clients who have engaged their own contractors — Shri Associates can act as a construction manager: coordinating schedules, supervising quality, and protecting the client's interests on site.

MEP Works Coordination

Electrical, plumbing, and HVAC coordination — working alongside specialist sub-contractors to ensure services are integrated cleanly with the architectural and structural design.
